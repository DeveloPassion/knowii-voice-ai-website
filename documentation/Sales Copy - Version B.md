## How It Actually Works (Technical Details)

Knowii Voice AI uses Whisper and Parakeet AI models.

These are state-of-the-art speech recognition models. The same technology that powers professional transcription services, but running locally on your computer.

You download the model once (2-10 GB depending on which model you choose).

When you record your voice, the audio stays on your computer and gets transcribed by the local AI model.

No internet required. No cloud API. No sending your voice anywhere.

If your computer has an NVIDIA GPU, transcription is accelerated and near-instant.

If you're on CPU-only, it takes a few seconds. Still fast enough to be useful.

You can choose between multiple models based on your priorities:

**Small models** - Fast, near-instant results, good accuracy

**Medium models** - Balanced speed and accuracy

**Large models** - Slower but more accurate, great for technical content or accents

Switch between them anytime. The app manages loading and unloading automatically.

## Common Questions

**Is this actually private?**

Yes. Everything runs on your computer. Your voice recordings are stored locally. The AI model runs locally. Nothing is sent to any server. You can run it with your internet connection disabled and it works perfectly.

**Does it work offline?**

Yes, completely. You need internet once to download the AI model (2-10 GB depending on the model). After that, it works offline forever.

**How accurate is it?**

Very. Whisper and Parakeet are among the best speech recognition models available. Accuracy depends on audio quality, background noise, and which model you use. Larger models are more accurate but slower. In practice, it's accurate enough for daily use without constant corrections.

**What languages work?**

All major languages: English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Dutch, Russian, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, and many more. You select your language in settings.

**Will this work on my computer?**

If you're running Windows 10/11 or Linux, yes. macOS support is coming. You don't need a powerful computer, but GPU acceleration helps (NVIDIA CUDA). It works fine on CPU-only systems, just a bit slower.

**What microphone do I need?**

Whatever you already have. Built-in laptop mics work. USB mics work. Headset mics work. Better microphones produce better results, but you don't need to buy anything special.

**Can I customize the keyboard shortcut?**

Yes. The default is Ctrl+Shift+G, but you can change it to whatever you want in settings.

**What about my clipboard?**

The app saves your clipboard before pasting, then restores it after. Your copied content isn't lost.

**How fast is transcription?**

With GPU acceleration and small models: near-instant (1-2 seconds). On CPU with larger models: 3-5 seconds. Fast enough to feel seamless in normal use.

**Can I use this at work?**

Yes. Commercial license included. Use it for personal projects, professional work, client communications, whatever you need.

**What if I have problems?**

Email me. I'll help. If something's broken, I'll fix it. If something's confusing, I'll explain it. You're not alone.

**Will the price increase?**

Yes. This is Early Access pricing. When the product leaves beta, the price will go up. Buy now to lock in $49 forever.
