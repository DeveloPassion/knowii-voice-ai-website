### Writing Style Notes

**Voice and tone:**
- Conversational, like explaining to a friend over coffee
- Reader-focused ("you" not "we")
- Specific and vivid (paint pictures)
- Honest about Early Access status
- Confident but not arrogant
- Warm but professional

**What works:**
- Short paragraphs (1-3 sentences)
- Generous line breaks (breathing room)
- Active verbs (speak, press, release, not "allows you to")
- Specific nouns (keyboard, cursor, clipboard, not "interface elements")
- Benefits over features (save time vs. fast processing)
- Questions as headers (mirrors reader's thoughts)

**What to avoid:**
- Exclamation points (used only 4 times in entire copy)
- Marketing jargon ("revolutionary," "game-changing," "innovative")
- Vague claims (specific numbers and examples instead)
- Feature lists without context
- Corporate speak
- Hype
